About

The Timagotchi is a low-cost device that helps with adhd time blindness with a progress bar, (inspired by this video) It tells you how much time you have left in classes, and also integrates with Canvas LMS to show grades and assignments.

Bill of Materials

  • Raspberry Pi Zero WH - $10
  • Waveshare 1.44" LCD HAT - $12
  • SD Card, Cable, Misc - Free/Under $5
  • Battery bank (at least 5kMAH) - $10-15
